Everything you need to know about LandlordHours and REPS qualification.

REPS is an IRS designation that allows real estate professionals to deduct rental property losses against ordinary income — without passive activity limitations. To qualify, you must spend at least 750 hours per year in real estate activities and more than half your total working hours in real estate. LandlordHours tracks both requirements automatically.
Qualifying activities include property management, maintenance and repairs, tenant screening, rent collection, bookkeeping for rental properties, researching new acquisitions, and travel between properties. LandlordHours organizes these into six IRS-aligned categories so every hour is properly classified.
Simply describe your work in natural language — for example, "Spent 2 hours fixing the kitchen faucet at Oak Street." The AI automatically determines the category (Repairs & Maintenance), assigns it to the right property, sets the duration, and fills in today's date. You just review and confirm.
