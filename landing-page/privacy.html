Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 28, 2026

LandlordHours ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

LandlordHours is designed with privacy at its core. The app stores all user data — including time entries, property information, and reports — locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any external server.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Financial or tax information
  • Property addresses or details
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes

AI-Powered Features

LandlordHours includes an AI-powered time entry feature. When you use this feature, the text you enter is processed to auto-fill form fields (category, property, hours, date). Any AI processing is performed locally or through privacy-preserving methods. We do not store, log, or use your input text for training or any other purpose beyond providing the immediate auto-fill result.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard data storage frameworks. Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features, including passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID if enabled. We have no access to your data.

Third-Party Services

LandlordHours does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third party for any reason.

Data Export and Deletion

You have full control over your data at all times:

  • You can export your data from within the app for your records
  • Deleting the app from your device permanently removes all associated data
  • No copies of your data exist on our servers because we do not collect your data

Children's Privacy

LandlordHours is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page, with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
