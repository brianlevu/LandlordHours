LandlordHours helps rental property owners track the work hours that matter for tax-year records.

Log time by property, category, date, and participant. Monitor Real Estate Professional Status, material participation, and short-term rental activity in one focused iPhone app.

Key features:

• Track hours by property and activity category
• Use AI-assisted entry to turn plain-English notes into structured time logs
• Monitor REPS 750-hour progress and the 50% rule
• Track 100-hour and 500-hour material participation goals
• Add spouse participation when it applies to your filing situation
• Import calendar events to speed up time logging
• Attach records and notes to time entries
• Export PDF reports for your CPA or tax records
• Back up records with iCloud sync when available

LandlordHours is built for organized recordkeeping. It does not provide tax, legal, or financial advice. Review your reports with a qualified tax professional.
