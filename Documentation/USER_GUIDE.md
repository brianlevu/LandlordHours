# LandlordHours - User Guide

## Getting Started

### What is REPS?
REPS (Real Estate Professional Status) is an IRS tax designation that allows investors to deduct rental real estate losses against other income. To qualify:
1. You must own at least 50% of the rental property
2. You must perform more than 750 hours of material participation annually
3. You must not have more than 500 hours in other trades/businesses

### Setting Up Your Account

#### Step 1: Add Your Properties
1. Go to **Properties** tab
2. Tap **+** to add a new property
3. Enter property name and address
4. Select type: **LTR** (Long-Term) or **STR** (Short-Term/Airbnb)
5. Tap **Save**

#### Step 2: Start Tracking Time
**Option A: Use the Timer**
1. On **Home** screen, tap **Start Timer**
2. Select your property
3. Choose activity category (repairs, management, etc.)
4. Tap **Start**
5. When done, tap **Stop & Save**

**Option B: Manual Entry**
1. Go to **Track** tab
2. Tap **Add Entry**
3. Select property, participant, category
4. Enter hours and date
5. Add notes if needed
6. Tap **Save**

### Understanding Categories

**These activities COUNT toward REPS:**
- 🛠 Repairs & Maintenance
- 📁 Property Management
- 🔑 Leasing & Tenant Relations
- 📊 Bookkeeping & Financial
- ⚖️ Legal & Compliance
- 🛡 Insurance & Claims
- 🚗 Travel to Property
- 🔨 Renovations & Improvements

**These do NOT count:**
- 📈 Investing Decisions
- 💰 Financing
- 📝 Contract Negotiation

### Checking Your Progress

**Home Screen shows:**
- Today's hours
- This week's hours
- Total REPS hours

**Reports Screen shows:**
- 750-hour progress ring
- Hours by property breakdown
- Hours by category breakdown
- 50% rule status

### Tips for Success

1. **Log in Real-Time** - Use the timer whenever you're working on your properties
2. **Be Specific** - Add notes describing what you did
3. **Keep Records** - Take photos of work done as evidence
4. **Check Monthly** - Review your progress at least monthly
5. **Plan Ahead** - Aim for 15+ hours/week to hit 750 hours

### Common Questions

**Q: Can my spouse's hours count toward REPS?**  
A: Yes! Both you and your spouse can log hours, and combined they count toward the 750-hour requirement.

**Q: What if I have multiple properties?**  
A: Add each property separately. Hours are tracked per property and combined for REPS compliance.

**Q: What happens if I don't hit 750 hours?**  
A: You won't qualify for REPS status, and rental losses may be considered passive (limited deduction).

**Q: Can I edit or delete entries?**  
A: Yes! Go to the History tab to edit or delete any time entry.
