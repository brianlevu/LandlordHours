What is Real Estate Professional Status?

5 min read · Updated Feb 2026
The Basics

Real Estate Professional Status (REPS) is an IRS designation that allows qualifying taxpayers to treat all rental real estate losses as non-passive. This means losses can offset your active income — W-2 wages, business income, etc.

💡 Key Requirements

  1. 750+ hours in real estate activities per year
  2. More than 50% of total working hours in real estate
  3. Material participation in each rental property
What Counts as RE Hours?

The IRS considers these qualifying activities: property management, repairs, tenant screening, bookkeeping, travel to properties, lease negotiations, and more.
